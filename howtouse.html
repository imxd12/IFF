How to Use

Mastering MoneyFlow

A fluid guide to the Antigravity Financial Engine.

1. The Hub (Dashboard)

The Hub is your central command center, providing a liquid financial overview in real-time. It continuously calculates your spending velocity and presents holistic analytics about your current monetary state.

  • Real-Time Cards: Instantly view Today's Expense, All-Time Balance, and customized monthly insights.
  • AI Voice Greetings: The app greets you contextually based on the time of day.
  • PWA Integration: Install MoneyFlow directly to your home screen via the prompt for a full-screen, native-app experience!

2. Advanced Category Manager

Control exactly how your finances are tagged. Navigate to the Settings -> Finance tab to access the powerful Category Manager.

  • Parent/Child Structure: Select a main category (e.g., Food) and directly add or edit specific subcategories (e.g., McDonald's, Groceries).
  • Custom Form UI: Enjoy beautiful, glassmorphic modal prompts for editing and deleting items, completely replacing ugly browser popups.
  • Instant Sync: Changes made here immediately update the dropdowns in both Spendly and PocketCal.

3. Spendly & PocketCal Engines

Log every transactional movement and map it across two powerful ledgers.

  • Spendly: Core analytics ledger. Swap between Income and Expense logging using the liquid toggle. Features live Chart.js visualizations.
  • PocketCal: A visual day-grid designed for allowances. Instantly see green rings for income days and red for expenses.
  • Magic Syncing: Adding an income labeled "💵 Pocket Money" inside Spendly magically bridges into your PocketCal balance.

4. Privacy Mode & Data Safety

Your data never leaves your device. MoneyFlow uses your browser's Local Storage exclusively.

  • Privacy Mode: Enable this in Settings to automatically blur your total balances on the dashboard. Simply hover over the blurred text to temporarily reveal your balance.
  • Offline First: Since everything is local, the app works entirely offline.
  • Complete Control: You can wipe your data completely from the Settings menu at any time.

5. Professional Passbook Export

Generate highly professional, HDFC-style PDF bank statements directly from your device.

  • Instant Compilation: Clicking "Export PDF" stitches your transactions into a standard A4 format without any cloud processing.
  • Dr/Cr Architecture: Automatically routes your flows into professional Debit and Credit structures alongside a calculated running balance.
  • Full Emoji Support: Category emojis are fully preserved in the PDF output using standard system fonts.

6. The Antigravity Ecosystem

MoneyFlow is governed by a localized physics engine, marrying structural layouts with fluid motions.

  • The Inky Theme Switch: Toggle between "Obsidian Black" and "Ceramic White" triggering an expanding clip-path sequence.
  • Tactile Haptics & Sound: The app physically taps your device's vibration motors and plays subtle UI sounds. You can adjust their intensity in Settings.

7. AI Smart Systems

MoneyFlow is powered by local AI to make your financial tracking effortless and proactive.

  • Smart Entry: Type naturally (e.g., "Got 500 from Dad") and the AI will extract the amount, date, and category automatically.
  • Anomaly Detection: If you accidentally log the same expense twice, the AI will catch it and ask for confirmation.
  • Predictive Alerts: MoneyFlow analyzes your spending velocity. If you are projected to overspend your income this month, you'll receive an early warning.
  • Streaks & Gamification: Build healthy financial habits. The AI tracks your "No Junk Food" or "Consistent Saving" streaks and rewards you with badges!
  • AI Health Report: Get instant, conversational insights summarizing your monthly performance.
