How to Use

Mastering MoneyFlow

A fluid guide to the Antigravity Financial Engine.

1. The Hub (Dashboard)

The Hub is your central command center, providing a liquid financial overview in real-time. It continuously calculates your spending velocity and presents holistic analytics about your current monetary state.

  • Real-Time Cards: Instantly view Today's Expense, All-Time Balance, and customized monthly insights.
  • AI Smart Insights: Neural-styled guidance and suggestions based on your logging frequency.
  • Quick Access: Navigate to any core feature (Spendly, PocketCal, Feedback) with a single tap.
  • PWA Integration: Install MoneyFlow directly to your home screen via the prompt for a full-screen, native-app experience!

2. Spendly Analytics

Spendly is your core ledger. Log every single daily transactional movement—whether it's an impromptu expense or an unexpected income.

  • Liquid Toggle: Seamlessly flip between Income and Expense logging using the centered liquid slider.
  • Categorizations: Assign universal tags (e.g., Food, Travel) for visual Chart.js mappings.
  • Dynamic Data Viz: Observe up to 4 real-time graphs recalculating instantly right as you log transactions.

3. PocketCal Engine

PocketCal translates arbitrary financial numbers into a chronological, scannable day-grid, originally designed for managing "Pocket Money" allowances but robust enough for anything.

  • Visual Day Grid: Instantly see which days you gained money (green rings) vs days you spent heavily.
  • Magic Syncing: When you add an income explicitly labeled "💵 Pocket Money" inside Spendly, it magically bridges over into your PocketCal balance.
  • Extreme Scaling: The UI is protected from layout-breakage, shrinking texts down intelligently to fit ANY device constraint globally.

4. The Inky Theme Switch

Why stick to one reality? MoneyFlow boasts a pristine light aesthetic ("Ceramic White") and a highly optimized dark mode ("Obsidian Black").

  • Liquid Expansion: Tap the Sun/Moon icon in the overarching header to initiate an expanding clip-path sequence spanning the whole DOM.
  • Engine Support: Uses the cutting-edge ViewTransitions API natively baked into modern browser pipelines.

5. Bank Passbook Exports

Sometimes you need official records—for parents, audits, or personal archiving. MoneyFlow computes real-time PDFs in your browser locally.

  • Instant Compilation: Clicking "Export PDF" instantly stitches your transactions into a standard A4 format without relying on a remote cloud.
  • Dr/Cr Architecture: Automatically routes your flows into professional Debit and Credit structures alongside running balances.

6. The Antigravity Ecosystem

MoneyFlow is completely governed by a localized physics engine, marrying structural layouts with fluid motions.

  • Scroll Physics: As you traverse sections, distinct cards float at unalike speeds using GSAP ScrollTrigger computations.
  • Feedback Mechanisms: If enabled inside your Settings, the app physically taps your device's vibration motors upon major interactions.
