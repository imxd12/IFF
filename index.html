Why MoneyFlow?

Redefining the way you interact with financial numbers through engineering and aesthetics.

100% Offline & Private

Your data never leaves your device. MoneyFlow operates strictly on a sandboxed local-first PWA architecture ensuring total cryptographically isolated privacy from the internet.

Liquid Interface UX

Built with a custom physics engine combining GSAP ScrollTriggers and lenis.js. Traverse a beautiful user interface that fluidly adapts to your gestures, bounces realistically, and scales perfectly.

Unified Analytics

Simultaneously log expenses via the Spendly Ledger and map overarching monthly budgets inside the PocketCal visual matrix. Everything magically syncs together instantaneously without loader spinners.
