Privacy Policy

Data Liquidization & Privacy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

MoneyFlow is a Progressive Web App (PWA). All your financial data, including income, expenses, and calendar entries, are stored strictly via LocalStorage on your device. We do not transmit your "Spendly" or "PocketCal" ledgers to any remote cloud servers. Because your device acts as the absolute database, data remains securely sandboxed within your browser's local state.

2. Antigravity Analytics

Any computations for Smart Insights, data visualization tools, and boxplots are executed on the client-side browser logic using JavaScript. Your data flows fluidly but remains localized.

3. Local File Generation

Our PDF Bank Passbook export features utilize 'jsPDF' to render the file dynamically in your browser. At no point is a file uploaded or passed through cloud services.

4. Clear Data Control

You have full control to wipe your financial history via the Settings panel. Triggering the wipe command irrevocably deletes the data shards from your local cache.
