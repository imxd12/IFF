At MoneyFlow, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal and financial information.

TL;DR: Your data stays on YOUR device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your financial information to our servers.

1. Information We DON'T Collect

MoneyFlow does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your financial transactions or balances
  • Bank account details or credit card information
  • Income or expense data
  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses or phone numbers (unless you contact us)
  • Browsing history or usage patterns
  • IP addresses or device identifiers

2. Local Data Storage

All your financial data is stored locally in your browser using:

  • localStorage: Browser-based storage that keeps data on your device only
  • No Cloud Sync: Data is never uploaded to any server or cloud service
  • Device-Specific: Data is tied to the specific browser and device you use
  • User Control: You can delete all data at any time using browser settings or Developer Options

Important: If you clear your browser cache or site data, all your MoneyFlow data will be permanently deleted.

3. Data You Can Choose to Share

The only data that leaves your device is when YOU explicitly choose to:

  • Export Data: When you click "Export CSV" or "Export JSON" buttons
  • Contact Us: When you send emails or messages to the developer
  • Share Screenshots: If you manually take and share screenshots of the app

These actions are completely voluntary and under your control.

4. Geolocation Data

MoneyFlow requests access to your location for the Profile page feature only:

  • Permission-Based: Location is accessed only with your explicit browser permission
  • Display Only: Location is shown on the Profile page but never stored or transmitted
  • Optional: You can deny location permission and the app will still work normally
  • No Tracking: We do not track your movements or location history

5. Third-Party Services

MoneyFlow uses the following third-party services that may have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts: Loads font files from Google's CDN (may collect basic usage data)
    Google Privacy Policy
  • Chart.js CDN: Loads charting library from jsDelivr (may log CDN requests)
    jsDelivr Privacy Policy
  • Browser APIs: Uses standard browser APIs (localStorage, Geolocation) governed by browser privacy policies

Note: These services are necessary for app functionality but do not have access to your financial data.

6. Cookies & Tracking

MoneyFlow does NOT use:

  • Cookies for tracking
  • Analytics services (no Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.)
  • Advertising networks or retargeting pixels
  • Session tracking or user profiling
  • Third-party tracking scripts

The only "cookie-like" storage is localStorage, which stores your app data locally for functionality purposes only.

7. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Local-Only Storage: Data never leaves your device, reducing security risks
  • No User Accounts: No passwords or login credentials to be compromised
  • No Server Storage: No central database that could be hacked
  • HTTPS Only: App should be served over HTTPS for encrypted connections
  • No External APIs: Financial data is never sent to external services

Your Responsibility: Keep your device secure with screen locks and don't share export files containing sensitive data publicly.

8. Children's Privacy

MoneyFlow does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for personal finance management and is suitable for all ages. Since no data is collected or stored on our servers, there are no special provisions for children's data.

9. Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data anytime within the app
  • Export: Download your data in CSV or JSON format
  • Delete: Clear all data using Developer Options or browser settings
  • Portability: Export and import data to move between devices
  • No Account Deletion Needed: Since we don't store your data, simply stop using the app

10. Progressive Web App (PWA) Installation

If you install MoneyFlow as a PWA on your device:

  • A service worker caches app files for offline access
  • Cached files are static assets (HTML, CSS, JS, images) only
  • Your financial data remains in localStorage, not in service worker cache
  • Uninstalling the PWA removes cached files but may not clear localStorage automatically
  • You may need to manually clear site data from browser settings after uninstalling

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12. International Users

MoneyFlow is accessible worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • No data crosses international borders through our servers
  • Your data remains subject to your local device and browser privacy settings
  • GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulations are largely not applicable since we don't collect personal data

14. Open Source & Transparency

MoneyFlow is committed to transparency:

  • The app code can be inspected using browser developer tools
  • You can verify that no network requests send your financial data
  • All localStorage operations can be monitored in browser DevTools
  • No obfuscated or minified code that hides functionality
