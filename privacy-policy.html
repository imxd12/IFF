Privacy Policy

Data Liquidization & Privacy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Storage & Local Sandboxing

MoneyFlow operates exclusively as a sandboxed Progressive Web Application (PWA) operating on a "Local-First" principle. This means absolutely zero transactional data, ledgers, or historical profiles are ever transmitted, synchronized, or stored on external cloud servers, databases, or remote endpoints.

  • All financial records are encrypted and retained entirely within your device's native browser localStorage API.
  • You maintain absolute sovereignty and physical ownership over your data footprints.

2. Application Telemetry & Analytics

MoneyFlow does NOT embed overarching tracking pixels, user behavior metrics, or third-party advertising monitors. The "Smart Insights" and numerical analytics generated on your dashboard are compiled deterministically on your local CPU via JavaScript math logic without invoking external analytics hubs.

3. External Libraries & CDNs

In order to deliver a high-fidelity visual experience, MoneyFlow fetches open-source stylistic and structural assets from trusted Content Delivery Networks (CDNs):

  • TailwindCSS: For responsive, inline stylistic computations.
  • Chart.js: For interpreting your local data points strictly inside the DOM canvas rendering pipeline.
  • GSAP & Lenis: Specifically allocated to handle the "Antigravity" scroll physics and fluid interface rendering matrices.
  • Lucide Icons & jsPDF: Essential for visual assets and local document compilation respectively.

4. Device API Utilization Requirements

With your explicit consent or activation toggles, MoneyFlow may request specific hardware-level APIs exposed via the browser:

  • Haptic Feedback / navigator.vibrate(): utilized exclusively for tactile confirmation of button taps and user interactions when explicitly enabled in Settings.
  • Speech Synthesis: utilized to deliver auditory AI greetings and status reads entirely without leveraging external Text-To-Speech cloud APIs.

5. Local Passbook Exports & Privacy Mode

MoneyFlow introduces advanced local rendering capabilities:

  • Offline PDF Generation: When you generate an HDFC-Style Passbook PDF, all compilation happens inside your browser using the `jsPDF` library. No transaction data is sent to a server to generate the document.
  • Privacy Mode: An optional setting that blurs your dashboard balances to protect your sensitive data from shoulder surfers.

6. Complete Data Erasure, Portability & Control

We implicitly strictly adhere to the fundamental principles of 'Right to Erasure' and 'Data Portability' without caveat:

  • Nuclear Wipe: A hard-delete function available persistently in User Settings to irrevocably destroy all locally cached financial logs and preferences.
  • JSON Portability: Users may freely extract their overarching application state via standard .json exports to secure their data offline or gracefully merge across their personal devices.
